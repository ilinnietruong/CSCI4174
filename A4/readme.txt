There are two java program:

standardACL.java

In the standardACL, the only thing you need to input are the 2 text files(line 22 and 23). The odd number at the end of standardTextFile is the act statements (standardTextFile1,standardTextFile3,standardTextFile5). The even number at the end of the standardTextFile are the source IP addresses(standardTextFile2,standardTextFile4,standardTextFile6).


extendedACL.java

In the extendedACL, the only thing you need to input are the 2 text files(line 33 and 34). The odd number at the end of extendedTextFile is the act statements (extendedTextFile1,extendedTextFile3,extendedTextFile5). The even number at the end of the standardTextFile are the source IP addresses, destination IP aaddresses and port number(extendedTextFile2,extendedTextFile4,extendedTextFile6).
